# Non-Technical Prompt Pack

## Setup
- `Start my invoice workspace for today.`
- `Load my vendor and PO records.`

## Ingestion
- `Get latest invoices from mail.`
- `Ingest from mail.`

## Review
- `Show invoices waiting for review in a table.`

## Alerts
- `Send slack alert of invoice 5.`
- `Send email alert of invoice 5.`

## Decisions (in chat, after team responds)
- `Approve invoice 5.`
- `Approve invoice 6 with note Verified bank change with vendor directly.`
- `Reject invoice 7 with note Unknown vendor, no PO on file.`

## Posting
- `Post invoice 5 to QBO.`
- `Post approved invoices to accounting.`

## Reporting
- `Generate month-end summary for 2026-04.`
- `Share month-end summary to Slack and email.`
